A WEBSITE FOR A CATALAN SONGWRITER


This is a website designed and developed for a Catalan songwriter - Albert Pastor. When Albert is preparing for his new album "L'INFINIT QUE SE ACABA'", he is looking for someone who can help with design and web development, so he found me.


This web is developed with WordPress template, also the task included a spaceship conceptual design for his album.


Tools:

  • WordPress
  • Elementor
  • CSS
  • Responsive Web
  • Autocad
  • Rhinoceros
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator

Experience

This time the experience was very special. As my client is an audiovisual technician and graphic designer, so he made the web design himself. And what he asked me to do is to develop the project with WordPress template with Elementor plugin, and also I was required to do a spaceship design for his lyrics book and website.


It actually took me more time on spaceship design, I spent a lot of time searching references online, finally I decided to use the figure of diamond as a prototype to design.


Website user stories:

  • Homepage: Displays the client's new album and his latest blogs
  • Biography: Tells Albert's story as a musician
  • Blog: Updating the musical activities
  • Discography: User can change his/her profile
  • Media: Shows the video edited by Albert
  • Contact

What I learnt

1. True Programmers Never Stop Learning

The truth is I didn't know WordPress before, so I spent one week watching tutorials and learning it, and one week later, I had WordPress under my belt. Like a Chinese say, "Learning is like rowing upstream: not to advance is to drop back". You must update in real time, especially in the programming world, having a good learning ability is vital.

2. Research from the beginning

I must thank Albert very much for giving me this special experience, although I am an architect, however I never thought of designing a spaceship. When you feel you have no idea where to start, doing sufficient research will help you find your way.
