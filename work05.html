A PRODUCT TUTORIAL FOR MATACHANA COMPANY


MATACHANA is an international company of technological solutions for the Healthcare and Life Science sectors. They were seeking a freelancer to develop a tutorial web for a product called "PHO Series".


To achieve those missions, I worked closely with their UX designer and technical director to create the whole page, which also supports multiple languages.


Tools:

  • Html5
  • CSS3
  • jQuery
  • Responsive Web
  • PHP
  • Adobe Illustrator

Experience

The website is made for - one of the MATACHANA sterilizable products - "HPO Series", the purpose is to guid its users to use this product by 4 steps and some essential tips, it's an amazing opportunity for me to collaborate with the MATACHANA team, and also I practiced my communication skills with the UX designer by this chance.


MATACHANA also required this webite to contain 6 languages, so I applied PHP to complete this mission.


User steps:

  • Step1: Preparing the load
  • Step2: Loading the sterilizer
  • Step3: Performing a cycle
  • Step4: Releasing the load
  • Product replacement

What I learnt

1. Development vs production

To ensure a good user experience to all users, I had to develop the project with the full supports of low version browsers and mobile devices, this experience is very valuable for my future projects.
